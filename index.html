How to Play!

  1. Move by touching or clicking the directional pad, or using the d-pad on your keyboard. Hold B to run and press A to switch genders.
  2. See if you can find Mewtwo on your own, or use the side-panel to activate the pathfinder.
  3. Using the pathfinder, you can find a path from any two points in the cave.
  4. Have fun!
